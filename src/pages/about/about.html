How to use this app

Follow these instructions so that you can be tracked on your ride!

Initial setup
  1. Make sure to complete your details in the settings page, including the Rider ID provided when you signed up to this event
  2. Click the save button so that the information is saved and sent to us
Before the ride
  1. Open the app and toggle the switch in the top right corner so that it is green, This means location tracking is on
  2. Check the odometer is set to 0, if it is not click on the odometer reset from the drop-down menu
  3. Click the play button in tbottom right corner to begin the location logging
  4. RIDE!
  5. Click the pause button in the bottom right, and toggle the switch in the top right after you are done with your ride to end tracking
Good luck and Be safe
